Hypertext Story
Red and the City
This project requires creating an original UI with no "next" buttons. The goal is to communicate to users that they have choices in this story where no images are allowed. Red and the City is a modernized and rewritten version of Little Red Riding Hood. The design focuses on emphasizing the color red and heart flowers. Many designs play around and correlate what is happening in the story.
No Mouse Project
History of the Internet
This Project is a website that demonstrates the history of the Internet guided by a computer avatar. This website stands out because the left and right arrow keys only control it. The avatar moves as the user press the moving key and walks through “history.”
Article Website
On Punctuation
On Punctuation is an article where the author expresses their feeling about how exciting or boring some punctuations are. The website is designed more handwritten to reflect how the paper is filled with opinions instead of facts.
Website as Game
Squid Game
Squid Game is a South Korean survival drama streaming television series created by Hwang Donghyuk and is one of the most trending shows on Netflix in 2021. For this project, we were expected to create a website game. I designed a website based on the fifth of the six games from Squid Game. The game is called Glass Stepping Stones, where the stepping stones are made from one of two types of glass, one will be tempered glass, and the other will be regular glass. The tempered glass stepping stones are strong enough to hold the weight of two people. However, the regular glass will break even if just one person steps on it. For this game, players will guess which one of the following two tiles is the tile made of the stronger tempered glass, and only step on those cross the eight pairs of tile. You may then cross over to the other side safely and pass this game.
